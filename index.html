[ THE BLANK CANVAS ]

a space where everyone is free to create whatever they can think of.

What is your relationship to the Blank Canvas?

 

Personally, blank canvases terrify me. I steer well clear of them. Staring at a blank canvas and deciding where to place the first paint stroke is by far the most difficult part of a project. It's also the most exciting. So I decided it was the perfect name for my business.

As a Creativity Coach, I love working with people who ooze colour and crave magic. Who have gifts to share but aren't sure what the next step is. Who are willing to partner with me on putting the final touches on their Life Masterpiece. Sound like anyone you know?


While I eventually envision having a large physical space in which to collaborate and connect with fellow creative souls, we shall have to make due in the interim with good old fashioned coffee shops and phone calls.
